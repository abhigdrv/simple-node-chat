1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how Flash Chat Pro ("we," "us," or "our") handles information when you use our anonymous messaging service ("Service"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our practices.

2. Information We Do NOT Collect

We want to be clear about what we DON'T collect:

  • Chat Messages: We do NOT store any messages on our servers
  • Personal Information: We do NOT require or collect email addresses, phone numbers, or real names
  • Tracking Data: We do NOT use cookies, tracking pixels, or analytics that identify you
  • Location Data: We do NOT collect precise geolocation information
  • Device Information: We do NOT collect detailed device fingerprints
  • Browsing History: We do NOT track your browsing behavior outside our Service

3. Information We Collect

3.1 Temporary Session Data

When you use Flash Chat Pro, we collect minimal technical information necessary for the Service to function:

  • Username: A self-generated username with random number (stored locally in YOUR browser, not our servers)
  • Connection Data: Temporary connection information via WebSocket to enable real-time messaging
  • IP Address: May be temporarily visible in server logs for technical operation (not stored long-term)

4. How We Use Information

The minimal technical data we process is used solely to:

  • Enable real-time message delivery between users
  • Maintain active connections for the duration of your session
  • Prevent abuse and maintain Service security
  • Comply with legal obligations when required

We do NOT use any data for:

  • Advertising or marketing
  • Selling to third parties
  • Building user profiles
  • Behavioral tracking

5. Data Retention

5.1 Message Retention

ZERO retention: Messages are transmitted in real-time and are NOT stored on our servers. Once delivered, they exist only in the recipients' browsers.

5.2 Technical Logs

Basic server logs (IP addresses, connection times) may be retained for:

  • Security purposes: Up to 7 days
  • Debugging: Up to 7 days
  • Legal compliance: As required by law

5.3 No Long-Term Storage

We do not maintain user databases, message archives, or long-term data storage of any kind.

6. Data Sharing and Disclosure

6.1 Third Parties

We do NOT share, sell, or rent any user data to third parties for commercial purposes.

6.2 Service Providers

We may use minimal third-party services for:

  • Hosting: Server infrastructure (no access to message content)
  • CDN: Content delivery for performance

6.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when legally required to:

  • Comply with valid subpoenas or court orders
  • Investigate suspected illegal activity
  • Protect the rights and safety of our users
  • Respond to law enforcement requests

Important: Since we don't store messages, we cannot provide message content even when legally compelled. We can only provide technical data that may be available in temporary logs.

We implement reasonable security measures including:

  • HTTPS/SSL: Encrypted connections for data in transit
  • WebSocket Security: Real-time encrypted communication
  • No Database: No message database means no database breaches
  • Minimal Attack Surface: Less data = less risk
